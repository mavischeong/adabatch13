Lille

Lille knows as Rijsel is a city in northern France, in French Flanders. On the Deule River, near France's border with Belgium, it is the capital of the Hauts-de-France region and the perfecture of the Nord department

As of 2009, Lille had a population of 226,827 within its adminstrative limits and an urban population of 1,015,744, making it the fourth largest urban area in France after Paris, Lyon and Marseille.
